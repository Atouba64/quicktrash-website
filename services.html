What We Haul

We pick up most non-hazardous items for your home or business.

What We Accept

Our contractors are equipped to handle a wide range of items. Use the app to specify your load size.

Household Waste

Standard garbage bags, food waste, and general refuse that doesn't fit in your weekly bin.

Bulk Items

Old furniture (sofas, chairs, tables), mattresses, box springs, and appliances (non-Freon).

Electronics (E-Waste)

Televisions, computers, monitors, and other electronic items for proper disposal.

Yard Waste

Bagged or bundled branches, leaves, grass clippings, and other landscaping debris.

Small Construction Debris

For small-scale DIY or renovation projects. This includes wood scraps, drywall pieces, and old fixtures.

Recyclables

Separated paper, cardboard, plastics, and glass, as per local guidelines.

What We Cannot Accept

For the safety of our contractors and to comply with state and local regulations, we cannot accept the following items:

  • Hazardous Materials (chemicals, paint, asbestos, solvents, etc.)
  • Medical Waste (sharps, biohazards)
  • Flammable or Explosive Materials
  • Any other items restricted by local or state law
