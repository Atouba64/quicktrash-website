Your Pickup Journey in 4 Steps

Step 1: Request Your Pickup

Open the Quick Trash app and select "New Pickup." You'll be guided to specify the type of trash (household, bulk, etc.) and the approximate size or quantity. You can also add a photo to help the contractor[cite: 15, 134, 136].

Step 2: Get Upfront Price & Pay

Based on your selections, our app will instantly provide a transparent, upfront price for the service. Confirm the details, and pay securely in the app to place your order. No haggling, no surprises[cite: 38, 137, 170].

Step 3: Contractor Accepts & Arrives

Your order is broadcast to the nearest available, vetted independent contractor[cite: 16, 171]. They will accept the job, and you'll receive a notification. We will aim to add real-time tracking in a future update[cite: 39, 140].

Step 4: Pickup & Responsible Disposal

Your contractor arrives, loads the trash, and takes a photo to confirm the pickup is complete[cite: 175]. They then transport the waste to one of our licensed partner facilities (like Waste Management or Republic Services) for legal and environmentally sound disposal[cite: 17, 40, 176].
