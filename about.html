Our Story

Quick Trash was founded to solve a common problem: needing to dispose of trash, bulk items, or project debris outside of regular collection schedules. Traditional services are often rigid and inconvenient. We leverage technology to connect people needing immediate trash removal with a network of local, independent contractors, providing a simple, flexible, and fast solution.

Our Mission

"To revolutionize trash pick-up by providing an immediate, reliable, and customer-centric on-demand service, ensuring convenient and responsible waste disposal for everyone."

Our Vision

"To be the leading on-demand trash pick-up service in Georgia, recognized for our speed, flexibility, and commitment to customer satisfaction and environmental responsibility, with plans for future expansion."
